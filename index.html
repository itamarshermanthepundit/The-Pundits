Step 3

Point system.

Simple scoring keeps the game easy to follow while still rewarding the big calls.

How points are earned

5

Correct group pick

Every correct group-stage prediction is worth 5 points.

20

Award picks

Champion, top scorer, and top assist provider are each worth 20 points if predicted correctly.

5

Round of 32 winner

In the last 32, players can pick the winner of every match. Each correct winning side is worth 5 points.

10

Exact score bonus

Players can also guess the exact score in each Round of 32 match. A perfect score prediction is worth 10 points.
